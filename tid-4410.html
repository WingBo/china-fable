我有一个梦想

我有一个梦想

在一片草原上

有一间自已的小屋

蓝天 碧水相伴

还有 属于我们的风

我会在屋前种一片梦想

每天清晨打开房门

静静守候每朵梦想的开放

我会为它们取一个相同的名字

叫做,守望幸福



i have a dream ：

on a grassland ,

a small house belongs to ourselves ，

which there is the blue sky and green water arround it

and the winds are part of us .

i will plant a vast of dreams which are front of the house ,

open the door every morning ,

and wait for every dream comes into bloom .

i will give them the same name :

expecting for felicity
